Story

In 1932, the Japanese army began conducting bacterial experiments in Qiqihar City, Heilongjiang Province. You are the main experimenter in the Japanese Army 516 Unit. The main job of the unit is to conduct bacterial experiments on human bodies to prepare for the subsequent "germ warfare". As you conduct experiments on humans again and again, your spirit becomes more and more... The weaker you became, and on August 15, 1945, after the Japanese army surrendered, you were tried as the defeated party, but you were imprisoned and sent back to a Japanese mental hospital for recuperation because of your poor mental state. After being sent back to China, after several After years of recuperation, your mental state slowly recovered. However, because the higher-ups needed experimental information, after seeing your mental state slowly recovering, they began to ask you to recall the details of the experiment. During questioning again and again, your mental state began to recover again. It got worse and even started to have many hallucinations.
